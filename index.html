Here’s where my story begins.


Design has always been a significant part of my life, deeply influenced by my family. My grandfather, a renowned designer in interior design and fashion, inspired me to explore the world of design and build my own path in this field. His legacy continues to guide my work, even today


My uncle, a talented painter and industrial designer, further shaped my journey. He encouraged me to pursue Industrial Engineering with a focus on system planning and analysis—a decision that equipped me with strong problem-solving abilities and an analytical mindset. These skills have been instrumental in understanding user needs and translating them into impactful designs.



Through collaboration with my partner, a skilled programmer, I also acquired valuable programming knowledge, which has significantly enhanced my UX design capabilities. Together, we tackled complex projects, and I gained firsthand experience in creating user-centric solutions.




In my previous roles, I contributed to projects that improved user engagement by 30% and streamlined workflows, reducing processing time by 20%. These experiences have helped me develop a strong foundation in crafting intuitive and efficient designs.





I’m excited about the opportunity to bring my skills and experiences to a new team, where I can combine my passion for design with my technical expertise to create exceptional user experiences. Let’s take a look at some of my work to see how I can make a difference.
